WHAT IS SLAVIC INTEGRATION WEEKEND?

Slavic Integration Weekend is an exceptional event that brings together people from different Slavic cultures in an atmosphere of friendship, cultural exchange, and shared fun.

During the weekend, participants will have the opportunity to meet new people, exchange cultural experiences, and participate in various integration activities. It's a perfect opportunity to build international friendships and broaden your horizons.

Our program includes both intensive integration time and moments of relaxation in beautiful surroundings. Workshops, team games, cultural evenings, and much more await you!
